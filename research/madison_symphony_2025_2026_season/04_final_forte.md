# 🎻 The Final Forte (2026 Bolz Young Artist Competition)

**Date:** March 4, 2026  
**Conductor:** John DeMain  
**Featured Finalists:**  
*   **Lorenz Fradkin-Annen** (Violin - Oregon High School)  
*   **Cordelia Momo Fredrickson** (Violin - Wisconsin Virtual Academy)  
*   **Zachary Lemke** (Marimba - Oregon High School)  
*   **André Peck** (Piano - Logan High School)  
**Venue:** Overture Hall, Madison, WI  

---

## 📖 Concert Background & Context
Held on Wednesday, March 4, 2026, *Wisconsin Young Artists Compete: The Final Forte* was the final round of the Madison Symphony Orchestra’s annual **Bolz Young Artist Competition**. The 2026 event marked the 20th anniversary of the statewide televised broadcast of the competition, which is produced in partnership with **PBS Wisconsin** and **Wisconsin Public Radio (WPR)**.

The competition is open to Wisconsin high school students (grades 9–12) who undergo rigorous preliminary and semi-final rounds. The four finalists perform as soloists with the full Madison Symphony Orchestra under the baton of John DeMain. The concert is broadcast live to a statewide audience. The first-place winner is awarded the **Marian Bolz Prize for Distinguished Musical Achievement** ($2,000 scholarship) and performs as a soloist the next day at the MSO's Spring Young People's Concert. The second-place winner receives the **Steenbock Youth Music Award** ($2,000 scholarship), and the remaining two finalists receive honorable mentions ($1,000 scholarships).

*   **Broad Appeal:** The broadcast production by PBS Wisconsin and WPR was lauded for its high visual and audio quality, bringing classical music into homes across the state and celebrating the artistic achievements of Wisconsin's youth. The live audience in Overture Hall was highly supportive, greeting each student with enthusiastic applause.
