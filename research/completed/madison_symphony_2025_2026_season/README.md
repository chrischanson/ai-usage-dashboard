# 🎻 Madison Symphony Orchestra: 2025–2026 Centennial Season

Welcome to the research workspace for the Madison Symphony Orchestra's (MSO) historic **100th Anniversary (Centennial) Season** (2025–2026). This landmark season marked a century of symphonic music in Madison, Wisconsin, and served as the final season for Music Director **John DeMain**, concluding his legendary 32-year tenure.
